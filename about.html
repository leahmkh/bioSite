All about Taylor!

Taylor is a determined and dedicated individual. She spent her formative yearsin the small Coast Guard community of Kodiak, Alaska. Surrounded by the cold beauty of Kodiak's mountains, Taylor developed a deep love for the ocean and an independent spirit. She spent many years after graduating high school as a commercial fisherwoman, braving the unpredictable and dangerous waters of the Bering Sea. These years gave her a very hard work ethic which eventually transferred over to welding.

Seeking a change of pace, Taylor joined the rest of her family in settling on the idyllic shores of Oahu. Soon after moving to the island, she welcomed a beautiful baby girl she named Ryder Bay. Now at the graceful age of 26, she and her daughter continue to constantly explore the island they call home.

Some of her favorite hikes include:

  • Diamond Head Trail
  • Koko Crater Trail
  • Olomana Trail
  • Wiliwilinui Trail
