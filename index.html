Welcome to Taylor!

Meet Taylor, a beautiful soul with an adventurous spirit. Taylor is 26 years old and has an unparalled love for the beaches in Hawaii. There is no place she would rather be than feeling warm sand and listening to the soothing sounds of waves. When she's not at the beach, you will probably find her cruising the scenic coastal roads on her bike, fully embracing the freedom the island of Oahu has to offer.

Her passion for activity doesn't stop there. Taylor is an avid fitness enthusiast and maintains her dedication to a healthy life style by going to the gym multiple times a week and hiking on various trails. She has proven success by losing 100lbs over the course of 18 months. Since she is in love with everything anime, her dream is to one day visit Japan.
